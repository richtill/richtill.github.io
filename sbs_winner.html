My Business Tips

When starting out, be wise with your money and don't fall into traps by using someone's services and not doing Due Diligence on them. Get feedback, look them up and request reviews from previous jobs, its not cheeky..... its being safe for your business. I did this and suffered the financial loss. Network - try a few but not too many. Once you find your group, engage and get to know them, who knows what opportunity's you can get or even future collaborations to make your business stronger. If you need help, look at those nearest to you. People love supporting new businesses, so don't be scared to ask for advise or help. Your network is there to support you, so use it.
